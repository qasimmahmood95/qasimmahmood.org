01 / about

Quality engineering where it can't be optional

I'm a Senior Software Development Engineer in Test with around seven years in regulated fintech, currently focused on digital asset custody. My work sits at the high-stakes end of quality: the release gates, contract coverage and compliance-critical test suites that stand between a regulated platform and a very bad day.

I work primarily in TypeScript with Playwright, and I test API-first — the UI is the last mile, not the foundation. That means deep coverage of the services that move and safeguard client assets, including the compliance flows (Travel Rule among them) that regulators actually audit.

More recently I've been leading AI adoption inside a regulated environment — running an internal advocacy initiative that gets AI tooling into engineers' hands with the guardrails a compliance-conscious firm needs. The interesting problem isn't whether AI speeds up testing; it's how to adopt it without weakening the controls the business depends on.

I hold an MEng from Imperial College London.
