Background

I'm a Senior Software Development Engineer in Test with around seven years in regulated fintech, currently focused on digital asset custody. Most of my work is on release gates, contract coverage and the compliance-critical test suites a regulated platform depends on.

I work primarily in TypeScript with Playwright and test API-first, with UI checks layered on top. In practice that means deep coverage of the services that move and safeguard client assets, including compliance flows such as the Travel Rule.

I also lead an internal AI advocacy initiative, helping engineers adopt AI tooling without weakening the controls a regulated firm has to maintain.

I came to software from engineering of a different kind: an MEng in Aerospace Materials from Imperial College London, then a graduate year examining superalloys bound for jet engines. Metallurgy and software quality turn out to have the same core question, which is how things fail under stress.
